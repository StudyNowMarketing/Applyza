Target Keywords

Applyza competes in a high-intent, high-stakes search category — international students actively searching for help will become enrolled students. The SEO opportunity is enormous and largely unclaimed at the source-market level: keywords like "study abroad consultants Nigeria", "how to study in UK from Kenya", and "UK student visa Morocco" have significant volume with weak competition from established players who focus on English-speaking markets. This strategy maps a clear path from the current ~28 public pages to over 300 indexed, search-optimised pages through a combination of destination landing pages, source-market pages, course-category pages, and a structured blog programme. Every page category is tied to the primary conversion: booking a free consultation.

2. Programmatic SEO Architecture
Four page types to scale from 28 pages to 200+ indexed pages
⚠️
CSR Note: Because Applyza is a React SPA (client-side rendered), programmatic pages will need either (a) an SSR migration to Next.js, or (b) pre-rendering via a tool like react-snap or a Vercel Edge function. Without server-side rendering, Googlebot may not see unique content on each programmatic page. This is the single highest-priority technical prerequisite before scaling to 200+ pages.
